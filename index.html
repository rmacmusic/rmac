A Message of hope

Robin Roberts, is a talented songwriter, singer, and writer from Richfield, Utah. She is known for using her musical gifts to inspire and uplift others. Robin performs with two talanted bands and has published a book titled "Keep Believing: True Testimonies That We Are Not Alone." As a faithful Christian, she shares her journey and faith through her music and writing, touching the lives of many..

Songs

Robin Roberts, with her melodious voice and heartfelt lyrics, has a way of painting vivid landscapes through her music. Her songs are an ode to the timeless beauty of the countryside and the genuine, heartfelt stories of its people. Keep Believing

Keep Believing: True Testimonies That We Are Not Alone captures authentic accounts from people of various denominations, highlighting God's presence amidst life's challenges. This collection of stories and testimonies reveals how God, spirits, and angels intervene in our lives, reminding us we're never alone. The book encourages wise choices and continued faith, offering peace and reassurance.
